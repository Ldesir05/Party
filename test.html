About

Footwrk a.k.a. The Bad Dansa Is an American Caribbean Artist / Entertainer from Brooklyn New York.  This artist managed to forge his style in the ultimate gray area.  Infusing Dancehall & hip-hop. “Dancehall & Hip-Hop was my way of life growing up listening to all style of Caribbean music at home and all Hip-Hop at school with friends; it’s so many other Caribbean Americans that feel the same way we should have to choose one over the other it’s who we are!”
